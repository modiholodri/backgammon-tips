#### The Art of Hitting: The Tempo Hit

A tempo hit sends an opponent's checker to the bar, forcing them to spend their next turn re-entering instead of building their position.
Even if the hit doesn't gain you a lasting material advantage, it buys you time.
Picture this: your opponent is one roll away from making their 5-point.
A well-timed hit makes them deal with the bar instead.
That's a tempo hit doing its job.
