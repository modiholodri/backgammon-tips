#### Bearing Off: Play for Perfect Rolls

While you can't control the dice, you can prepare for them. Think ahead: if you roll double sixes, do you have enough checkers on the 6-point to make use of them? 
If not, adjust your setup beforehand. The same goes for double fours, fives, or twos. 
A well-distributed board lets you fully capitalise on any doubles - the dream of every backgammon player.
