#### Priming vs. Blitzing

Made points serve two different attacking strategies. Priming creates a wall of consecutive points to contain opponent checkers.
Blitzing hits blots and closes home board points to keep the opponent on the bar.
Here's a nice thing: even when a blitz stalls, those points you made during the attack often form a wall that traps the opponent's checkers.
So your effort is never wasted.
