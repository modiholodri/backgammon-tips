#### The Blitz

A blitz is an all-out attacking strategy. The goal is to hit the opponent's checkers, trap them on the **bar**,
and close your **home board** before they can re-enter. A successful blitz wins **gammons**.

**When to blitz**: the opponent has exposed blots in or near your home board, and you have **builders** ready to close points.

**How to execute**: hit every chance you get. Make home board **points**. Don't worry about leaving your own blots in the process:
a blitz is high-risk, high-reward, and hesitating usually costs more than committing.

If the blitz stalls (the opponent anchors or you run out of builders), the game often transitions into a priming game. 
The points you made during the attack become a wall that traps the opponent's checkers.
So even a blitz that doesn't finish the job can leave you in great shape.
