#### Defensive Fundamentals: The Stacking Trap

It's really common to avoid blots by stacking extra checkers on points you already own.
It feels safe in the moment, but it's actually a trap.
Stacking creates tall, inflexible columns (sometimes called candlesticks) that waste checkers.
Five checkers on the 6-point gives you one safe point and four checkers doing nothing.
Spreading those checkers out to make new points is stronger, even if it means leaving temporary blots.
