#### Which Strategy to Use?

The position tells you. Check the pip count, look at where the checkers are, and see what the dice allow.
- If you're ahead in the race with no contact, run.
- If the opponent has blots in your home board, blitz.
- If you have consecutive points trapping their checkers, prime.
- If you're behind with a good anchor, hold.
- If you're far behind with deep anchors, play a back game.
