#### For Beginners, Split or Slot?

Split. Splitting is lower risk and creates useful options on future turns. 
Slotting is the more aggressive play: it pays off when you cover the blot next turn, but it costs you when the opponent hits it. 
If you're still getting comfortable with the game, go with splitting.
It's the more reliable approach, and you can always add slotting to your toolkit later.
