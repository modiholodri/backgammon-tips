#### The 25% Rule

When someone doubles you, here's a handy way to think about it: if your chance of winning the game is above 25%, taking is better than dropping in the long run. 
You're risking 1 point (the current stake) to play for 3 (your opponent's 2 points plus your 1 at the new stake).
At 25% win probability, the risk and reward balance exactly. Below 25%, drop. Above 25%, take.
You don't need to calculate this precisely. Just ask yourself, "Can I win roughly one time in four from here?"
