#### Perfectly Position Your Anchors

Getting your anchors in the best possible position is vital when playing a back game. Deep anchor positions are the most favorable for this strategy.
So, establishing your anchors as deep as you can on your opponent's home board is advisable.
The aim of doing this is to maximize opportunities that allow you to hit their blots as the game progresses.

The best positions to secure favorable hitting opportunities are the first three points on your opponent's home board. 
So, establishing two of your anchors in these deep positions is ideal.
