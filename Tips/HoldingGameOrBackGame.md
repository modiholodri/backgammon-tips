#### Holding Game or Back Game?

- A **holding game** uses one advanced anchor (the 20-point or 21-point) to wait for a shot while racing the rest of your checkers home.
- A **back game** uses two or more deep anchors (the 1, 2, or 3-points) and relies entirely on hitting a late shot.

The back game is riskier and is typically a last resort when you're far behind.
